Welcome to my choose your own path game!

Here you will read the story, and make decisions that will affect the outcome.

Let's go!

You open your eyes to nothingness. It's dark, extrmely dark. You look down at your feet and see grass. There's a slight, cool breeze so you suspect you're outside. You don't remember how you got here. You only know two things: you're hungry, and alone. You have a choice to make. Do you crawl slowly on the grass and hope to find something? Or do you run like the wind and hopefully make it to a building or a town?

What do you do?
